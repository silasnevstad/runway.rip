Select your preferred email provider using the switch below. This guide will show you how to set up emails using either [Resend](https://resend.com) or [Mailgun](https://www.mailgun.com).

        ### <div className="flex items-center"><WrenchIcon className="h-6 w-6 mr-2 text-primary-500" /> Setup</div>
        1. Sign up for a [Mailgun](https://signup.mailgun.com/new/signup) account.
        2. Go to <InlineHighlight type="navigation">Send</InlineHighlight> > <InlineHighlight type="navigation">Sending</InlineHighlight> > <InlineHighlight type="navigation">Domains</InlineHighlight> and click <InlineHighlight type="button">Add New Domain</InlineHighlight>. Consider creating a specific subdomain, such as mail.yourdomain.com, for your email service.
        3. Complete the DNS verification process by adding the DNS records provided by Mailgun to your domain's DNS settings.
           <Info>
               This could take up to 48 hours but usually happens within a few minutes.
           </Info>
        4. Click your account in the top right and select <InlineHighlight type="navigation">API Security</InlineHighlight> > <InlineHighlight type="button">Add new Key</InlineHighlight>, then add this to the `.env.local` as `MAILGUN_API_KEY`.
        5. Add the domain you verified to the `.env.local` as `MAILGUN_DOMAIN`.
